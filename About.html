welcome to our services

Healthy Walk enterprise is a small business which manufactures high quality slippers for indoors. The healthy slippers are authentic since they are handcrafted by our most skilled craftsmen and are infused with soft fur to guarantee maximum for kind of foot. Our slippers absorb shocks and vibrations better than other types of slippers and they are very durable. Slippers can keep on safe from bacteria and fungal diseases, they nurture swollen feet and reduces chances of falls and drips inside the house. If you are suffering foot ache and face difficulty in doing your daily routine, our quality handcrafted slippers can reduce pain and discomfort. Our aim is to provide our customers with quality slippers for those relaxed indoor coffee moments, that can help them walk easy indoors. One thing that gives us a competitive edge over our competitors is we extra thick layers with foam absorption capabilities for our slippers which makes last longer. Healthy walk enterprise is founded by six partners who contributed capital equally and share profits and losses equally. The enterprise has been in operation for five days and so has got 9 confirmed orders and six on standby.
